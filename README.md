## Purpose

This repository helps users organize materials when a centralized exchange, trading platform, wallet service, or decentralized service front-end requests additional review or restricts account functions.

It is designed for documentation preparation, factual explanation, and compliance-oriented communication. It is not intended to bypass platform controls, misrepresent facts, or guarantee any review outcome.

## When to Use This Kit

This kit may be useful when a platform shows messages such as:

- Account restricted or under review
- Withdrawal suspended, delayed, or under risk review
- P2P/C2C function restricted
- Source of funds requested
- Additional KYC or enhanced due diligence required
- Wallet address flagged by compliance screening
- Trading, deposit, withdrawal, API, or account functions temporarily limited

For decentralized services, similar issues may appear as front-end access restrictions, wallet risk flags, third-party compliance screening results, or service-provider limitations.

## What This Kit Helps You Prepare

- Account and restriction summary
- Timeline of relevant events
- Source of funds explanation
- Transaction and order records
- On-chain transaction hashes
- Screenshots of platform notices
- Customer support ticket history
- Evidence package index
- Appeal email or support-ticket draft
