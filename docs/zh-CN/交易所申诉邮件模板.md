# 交易所申诉邮件模板

## 目的

本模板用于在已经整理好基础材料后，向交易所客服、合规审核或风控团队提交简明的账户审核/申诉说明。

## 适用场景

当账户功能被限制、提现审核、平台要求补充资料或资金来源说明时，可以参考本模板。

## 常见错误

- 只表达情绪，不提供事实和附件。
- 威胁客服或反复创建多个矛盾工单。
- 没有证据就声称资金一定没有问题。
- 忘记提供 UID、工单号、限制类型或平台提示原文。

## 模板

标题建议：

```text
Request for Account Review and Restriction Clarification - UID: [你的 UID]
```

正文模板：

```text
Dear Compliance / Risk Review Team,

I am writing to request a review of the current restriction placed on my account.

Account Information:

- Platform:
- Account UID:
- Registered email:
- Affected function:
- Date of restriction:
- Ticket / case / appeal ID:

The account currently shows the following notice:

[粘贴平台提示原文]

The affected function is:

- Withdrawal
- Deposit
- Trading
- Login
- P2P/C2C
- API
- Other: ________

I understand that the platform may require additional information for compliance and risk-control purposes. I have prepared the following materials for your review:

1. Account and restriction summary
2. Timeline of relevant events
3. Source of funds explanation
4. Transaction records and order screenshots
5. On-chain transaction hashes, where applicable
6. Supporting documents related to the relevant funds
7. Customer support or counterparty communication records, where applicable

I respectfully request that the review team examine the attached materials and advise whether any further information is required.

I understand that review outcomes are subject to the platform's internal policies, compliance obligations, and risk-control procedures.

Thank you for your time and review.

Sincerely,

[姓名]
[联系方式]
```

## 中文说明

这封邮件的重点不是施压，而是让审核人员快速看到：

- 你是谁
- 哪个平台账户
- 哪个功能被限制
- 限制什么时候发生
- 你已经准备了哪些材料
- 你愿意配合补充审核

不要写：

- “必须马上解冻”
- “我保证资金没问题”但不提供证据
- “不处理我就曝光”
- 与前后材料矛盾的解释

建议把附件整理成清晰编号，例如 `A01-账户限制提示截图.pdf`、`C01-资金来源说明.pdf`、`D01-交易哈希列表.xlsx`。

## 官方参考

请查看 [官方参考链接](官方参考链接.md)。如果交易所有专门的申诉入口或表格，应优先使用官方入口。

## 重要声明

本模板不承诺账户恢复、提现通过或资金释放。最终结果由相关平台审核决定。
