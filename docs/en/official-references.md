## Common Mistakes

- Relying on screenshots from unofficial social media accounts instead of official support pages.
- Following third-party instructions that promise guaranteed unfreezing or withdrawal approval.
- Using outdated help-center content without checking the latest account notice.
- Sending sensitive documents to people who are not part of the official platform support flow.

## Important Notice

Exchange rules and support flows change. Always follow the latest instructions shown inside your official account and the official help center of the relevant platform.

This repository summarizes documentation-preparation logic only. It does not represent, replace, or override any official exchange process.
