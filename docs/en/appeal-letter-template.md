# Appeal Letter Template

## Purpose

Use this template to draft a concise account-review or appeal message after you have prepared supporting materials.

## When to Use This

Use this template when a platform has restricted an account function, delayed a withdrawal, requested additional review, or asked for source-of-funds information.

## Common Mistakes

- Sending an emotional message without facts or attachments.
- Threatening support staff or creating multiple conflicting tickets.
- Claiming guaranteed legitimacy without evidence.
- Omitting UID, ticket ID, restriction type, or exact platform notice.

## Template

Subject: Request for Account Review and Restriction Clarification - UID: [Your UID]

Dear Compliance / Risk Review Team,

I am writing to request a review of the current restriction placed on my account.

## Account Information

- Platform:
- Account UID:
- Registered email:
- Affected function:
- Date of restriction:
- Ticket / case / appeal ID:

## Restriction Summary

The account currently shows the following notice:

> [Paste the exact wording shown by the platform]

The affected function is:

- [ ] Withdrawal
- [ ] Deposit
- [ ] Trading
- [ ] Login
- [ ] P2P/C2C
- [ ] API
- [ ] Other: __________

## Materials Prepared

I understand that the platform may require additional information for compliance and risk-control purposes. I have prepared the following materials for review:

1. Account and restriction summary
2. Timeline of relevant events
3. Source of funds explanation
4. Transaction records and order screenshots
5. On-chain transaction hashes, where applicable
6. Supporting documents related to the relevant funds
7. Customer support or counterparty communication records, where applicable

## Request

I respectfully request that the review team examine the attached materials and advise whether any further information is required.

I understand that review outcomes are subject to the platform's internal policies, compliance obligations, and risk-control procedures.

Thank you for your time and review.

Sincerely,

[Name]

[Contact Information]

## Important Notice

This draft does not guarantee account reinstatement, withdrawal approval, or fund release. Review outcomes are controlled by the relevant platform.
