# Main Exchange Handling Notes

This page summarizes practical preparation points for common platforms. It does not replace official exchange instructions. Always follow the latest notices shown in your account and the platform's official help center.

## Binance

Common situations may include withdrawal review, account restriction, P2P function limits, enhanced due diligence, source of funds requests, or risk-control checks.

Before submitting an appeal or contacting support, prepare:

- Binance UID
- Affected function: withdrawal, spot, futures, P2P, login, API, fiat, or other
- Exact restriction message
- Appeal button or support ticket screenshot, if available
- Related order IDs
- Deposit and withdrawal records
- TxID / transaction hash
- Source of funds explanation
- Screenshots of identity, payment, or order records requested by Binance

Official reference: https://www.binance.com/en/support

## OKX

Common situations may include account review, withdrawal review, C2C/P2P restrictions, locked assets, risk-control checks, or additional verification requests.

Before submitting an appeal or contacting support, prepare:

- OKX UID
- Affected function: withdrawal, C2C/P2P, trading, login, API, fiat, or other
- Lock or restriction type shown in the account
- Order ID or asset record
- Payment proof
- Counterparty communication, where relevant
- Source of funds explanation
- Screenshots of restriction notices and support replies

Official reference: https://www.okx.com/help

## Bybit

Common situations may include withdrawal review, P2P order disputes, account risk review, additional verification, or restricted account functions.

Before submitting materials, prepare:

- Bybit UID
- Restricted function and exact message
- Order ID, P2P order ID, or transaction record
- Deposit and withdrawal records
- TxID / transaction hash
- Payment proof and counterparty communication, if P2P-related
- Source of funds explanation
- Timeline of actions before the restriction

Official reference: https://www.bybit.com/en/help-center/

## Coinbase

Common situations may include account under review, restricted sending/receiving, identity verification review, source of funds checks, or compliance-related holds.

Before contacting support, prepare:

- Coinbase account email
- Affected function: buy, sell, send, receive, cash out, login, or other
- Exact message shown in the app or web account
- Date range of the restriction
- Transaction IDs or blockchain transaction hashes
- Identity verification status
- Source of funds explanation
- Screenshots of official notices

Official reference: https://help.coinbase.com/

## Kraken

Common situations may include account verification review, withdrawal hold, funding review, source of funds request, or compliance checks.

Before replying to support, prepare:

- Kraken public account ID or ticket number
- Affected function
- Exact platform notice or support request
- Funding records
- Trade and withdrawal history
- Source of funds explanation
- Supporting documents requested by Kraken

Official reference: https://support.kraken.com/

## Gate, KuCoin, Bitget, MEXC, HTX and Other Platforms

For other platforms, the same preparation logic applies:

- Account UID
- Affected function
- Exact restriction message
- Ticket or appeal number
- Related order and transaction records
- On-chain transaction hashes
- Source of funds explanation
- Payment and counterparty evidence
- Screenshots of all platform notices

Official references:

- Gate Help Center: https://www.gate.com/help
- KuCoin Help Center: https://www.kucoin.com/support
- Bitget Support: https://www.bitget.com/support

## Important Reminder

Do not rely on anyone who claims they can bypass exchange risk control, guarantee withdrawal approval, or remove restrictions without platform review. Most legitimate resolution paths require factual explanation, identity/account verification, source of funds documentation, and platform-side review.
